## Tracker CSV Format

```csv

```

States: `da_inviare`, `inviato`, `risposta`, `demo`, `beta`, `converted`, `no_risposta`

## Channel Priority (Italian B2B)

1. Instagram DM — shortest, works for restaurants
2. LinkedIn DM — more formal, works for professionals
3. Email — from restaurant's website, highest effort but most professional
4. Follow-up after 5 days if no response

## Rules

- Never mention price in first contact
- Personalize with restaurant name when possible
- Always offer 30 days free trial
- Target: restaurants with 20+ seats in Turin
- Volume: 10 contacts/day = 300/month
- Expected conversion: 5% response → 30% demo → 40% convert = ~2 clients/month
